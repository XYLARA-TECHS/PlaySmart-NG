Data Plans & Internet Tips

Save your data, choose the right plan, and stay connected ⚡

MiFi vs Phone Hotspot

• MiFi: Dedicated device for stable Wi-Fi for multiple devices
• Phone Hotspot: Convenient, but drains battery and may be slower
Recommendation: Use MiFi if you game or stream often.

Data Saving Tips

• Use Wi-Fi whenever possible
• Download games and videos on Wi-Fi
• Enable data saver in apps
• Limit background app data usage

NOTE: APPLICABLE FOR BOTH LAPTOPS, COMPUTERS AND MOBILE PHONE
