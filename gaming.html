Gaming Guides & Data Usage

Everything you need to play smart ⚡

Game Data Usage

Call of Duty Mobile: ~35MB - 100MB per hour
Free Fire: ~10MB - 30MB per hour
PUBG Mobile: ~20MB - 50MB per hour
eFootball: ~40MB - 150MB per hour

Best Emulators

BlueStacks – Best for mid-to-high-end laptops with good RAM and CPU
GameLoop – Best for gaming-focused PCs, especially for Tencent games
LDPlayer – Lightweight option for low-end laptops or older devices

Which to Use?

  • 💻 Low-end Laptop → LDPlayer
  • 🖥️ Mid-range Laptop → Gameloop
  • 🎮 High-end Laptop → BlueStacks

NOTE: GameLoop can also run on mid-range laptops (8GB RAM+, Core i5 / Ryzen 5+, SSD recommended)

Performance Tips

• Close background applications to reduce lag
• Lower game graphics if your device is low-end
• Use a stable Wi-Fi network for smooth gameplay
